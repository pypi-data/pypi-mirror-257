Welcome to XspecT and ClAssT!


XspecT is a tool to taxonomically assign bacterial Input-Data using Bloom Filters. XspecT performs a taxonomic assignment on the Species-Level while ClAssT, which is integrated into XspecT, uses Strain-Typing to identify International-Clones of A.baumannii. XspecT is also capable of screning for existance of Oxa-families.
For more information about the tool please visit our information-page.
